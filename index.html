Delicious Pancakes

Nothing beats the taste of homemade pancakes for breakfast! This easy pancake recipe is quick to prepare and results in fluffy, golden-brown pancakes every time. Serve with syrup and your favorite toppings.

Fun Fact: Did you know pancakes date back to Ancient Greece?

You can experiment by adding blueberries, chocolate chips, or bananas to your pancake batter for even more deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
